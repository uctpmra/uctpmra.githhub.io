Welcome to Help Coral Reefs Website!

Hello Everybody!

I am Azrin, an eight-grader at the Belmont Ridge Middle School in Ashburn, Virginia. Thanks for visiting my website Help Coral Reefs. I have built this website to promote awareness in our society about the importance of saving coral reefs. I hope you find the information in this website useful.

This website is all about promoting dialogues and exchanging ideas about how together as a community we can protect the coral reefs. So please email me your ideas, information, or questions about protecting the coral reefs at xx@gmail.com. I will be happy to post those in this website.
